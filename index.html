What is the Jar?

The Jar is the logo for the 100% workshop technology conference taking place at the end of summer 2015 at Warsaw, Poland. If you believe in learning by doing approach and are thirsty for knowledge and networking this conference is for you! There will be multiple hands-on sessions on software development, devops tools, product design, IoT and hardware renaissance, agile practices and many many more. Instead of just talking about great new tools - the Jar will show you how to actually use them.

Why we need the name for the Jar?

Because the Jar is full of knowledge and ideas, but no-one knows how to call it. Do you?
